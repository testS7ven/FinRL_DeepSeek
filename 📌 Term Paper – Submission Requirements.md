# 📌 Term Paper – Submission Requirements

Dear Students,

Thank you all for your participation in the course.

 Your term paper submission consists of four required components.

Please make sure that all items are submitted before the deadline of **May 5th**

If you have questions, please ask on Discord: [https://discord.gg/GbdbN5FTZ](https://discord.gg/GbdbN5FTZ)

or post an issue on GitHub: [https://github.com/benstaf/Aivancity\_course](https://github.com/benstaf/Aivancity_course)

# 1️⃣ Paper for NeurIPS submitted to OpenReview

Format your paper using the official NeurIPS Latex template:

[https://www.overleaf.com/latex/templates/neurips-2024/tpsbbrdqcmsh](https://www.overleaf.com/latex/templates/neurips-2024/tpsbbrdqcmsh)

Create an OpenReview profile using your AIvancity email:   
[https://docs.openreview.net/getting-started/creating-an-openreview-profile](https://docs.openreview.net/getting-started/creating-an-openreview-profile)

Submit your manuscript to OpenReview before May 5th: [https://openreview.net/group?id=NeurIPS.cc/2026/Conference\#tab-recent-activity](https://openreview.net/group?id=NeurIPS.cc/2026/Conference#tab-recent-activity)

# 2️⃣ HAL Submission with Cross-Post to arXiv

Submit your paper to HAL: [https://doc.hal.science/compte-et-profil/](https://doc.hal.science/compte-et-profil/)

Enable cross-posting to arXiv: [https://doc.hal.science/en/deposer/transfert-hal-arxiv/](https://doc.hal.science/en/deposer/transfert-hal-arxiv/)

Provide the HAL submission link in your final materials.

# 

# 3️⃣ GitHub Repository

You must provide a link to a public GitHub repository containing your code

Create a GitHub account: [https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/start-your-journey/creating-an-account-on-github](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/start-your-journey/creating-an-account-on-github)

Upload a project: [https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/start-your-journey/uploading-a-project-to-github](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/start-your-journey/uploading-a-project-to-github)

# 4️⃣ ChatGPT, Claude, Grok and Gemini Review Links

For strict quality control, you must also include 4 public links to conversations with: ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, and Gemini Think, starting with the following prompt:

\[Paste your article in LaTeX\] Here is my draft. Is it ready for submission to NeurIPS 2026, or should I revise it before, and how? Write a NeurIPS-level review of my draft, based on NeurIPS evaluation criteria and reviewer guidelines:  
[https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2015/PaperInformation/EvaluationCriteria](https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2015/PaperInformation/EvaluationCriteria)  
[https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2025/ReviewerGuidelines](https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2025/ReviewerGuidelines)

If any of the 4 chatbots does not recommend submission, improve your draft and iterate as many times as necessary 

How to share conversation links:

ChatGPT:   
[https://help.openai.com/en/articles/7925741-chatgpt-shared-links-faq](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/7925741-chatgpt-shared-links-faq)
